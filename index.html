What is EcoViewer

Ecological context,
at any scale

EcoViewer brings together high-resolution environmental datasets from multiple sources into a unified reference platform — designed for ecologists, remote sensing specialists, and conservation practitioners.

The integrated GBIF layer maps species occurrence data directly to ecosystem types, bridging biodiversity records with landscape-level ecological context.
