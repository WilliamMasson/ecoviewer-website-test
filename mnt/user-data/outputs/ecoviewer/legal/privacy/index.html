Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

EcoViewer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and handle information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

EcoViewer does not collect personal information directly. As the Service is built on Google Earth Engine, your use is subject to Google's data practices, which may include authentication info, usage data, and technical data such as IP address and browser type.

3. Website Analytics

This website may use anonymised analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. No personal data is stored on our servers.

4. Cookies

This website uses essential cookies only. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

5. Third Parties

When you use the EcoViewer application, you interact directly with Google's systems and GBIF. We encourage reviewing Google's Privacy Policy and GBIF's terms.

6. Data Retention

EcoViewer does not store personal data on its own servers.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data. As EcoViewer does not directly hold personal data, requests should be directed to the relevant third-party provider.

8. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact the team via the Team page.
