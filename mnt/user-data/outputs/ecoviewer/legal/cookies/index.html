Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website, used to make websites function and provide information to site owners.

2. How EcoViewer Uses Cookies

The EcoViewer website uses a minimal number of cookies for essential functionality only. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking.

3. Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies — Necessary for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies (if applicable) — Anonymised analytics only. No personally identifiable information. Can be disabled without affecting site functionality.

4. Third-Party Cookies

When using the EcoViewer application (powered by Google Earth Engine), Google may set its own cookies per their Privacy Policy. Google Fonts used on this site may also make requests to Google's servers.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
