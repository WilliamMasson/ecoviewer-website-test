01 — Environmental Datasets

Data sources used in EcoViewer

Imagery

Sentinel-2 Multispectral Imagery

High-resolution (10m) multispectral imagery from ESA's Sentinel-2 constellation, providing global coverage with frequent revisit times for land surface monitoring.

ESA Copernicus · 10m resolution · Global
Imagery

Landsat Collection 2

USGS Landsat archive providing decades of consistent multispectral observations for land cover change analysis and long-term ecosystem monitoring.

USGS / NASA · 30m resolution · Global · Since 1972
Elevation

SRTM Digital Elevation Model

NASA's Shuttle Radar Topography Mission providing near-global terrain elevation data at 30m resolution — essential for topographic context in ecosystem characterisation.

NASA · 30m resolution · 60°N–56°S
Vegetation

MODIS Vegetation Indices (MOD13)

NASA MODIS-derived NDVI and EVI products providing vegetation phenology and productivity metrics at global scale since 2000.

NASA · 250–500m resolution · Global · 16-day composites
Climate

WorldClim v2 / CHELSA

High-resolution global climate surfaces including temperature, precipitation, and 19 bioclimatic variables, critical for biome delineation and species distribution modelling.

WorldClim / CHELSA · ~1km resolution · Global
Land Cover

ESA WorldCover 2021

Global land cover classification at 10m resolution based on Sentinel-1 and Sentinel-2 data, providing 11 land cover classes.

ESA · 10m resolution · Global · 2020–2021
Land Cover

Dynamic World (Google / WRI)

Near real-time global land use / land cover dataset at 10m resolution, updated with each Sentinel-2 overpass.

Google / WRI · 10m resolution · Global · Near real-time
Biodiversity

GBIF Species Occurrences

Global species occurrence records from the Global Biodiversity Information Facility, mapped to ecosystem types for biodiversity-informed ecological characterisation.

GBIF · Global · 2.5+ billion occurrence records
