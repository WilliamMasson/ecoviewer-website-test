// Data Sources

Imagery

Sentinel-2 Multispectral Imagery

High-resolution (10m) multispectral imagery from ESA's Sentinel-2 constellation, providing global coverage with frequent revisit times for land surface monitoring.

Imagery

Landsat Collection 2

USGS Landsat archive providing decades of consistent multispectral observations for land cover change analysis and long-term ecosystem monitoring.

Elevation

SRTM Digital Elevation Model

NASA's Shuttle Radar Topography Mission providing near-global terrain elevation data at 30m resolution — essential for topographic context in ecosystem characterisation.

Vegetation

MODIS Vegetation Indices

NASA MODIS-derived NDVI and EVI products providing vegetation phenology and productivity metrics at global scale with consistent temporal coverage.

Climate

WorldClim / CHELSA Climate Data

High-resolution global climate surfaces including temperature, precipitation, and bioclimatic variables, critical for biome delineation and species distribution modelling.

Land Cover

ESA WorldCover & Dynamic World

Global land cover classifications at 10m resolution providing baseline ecosystem-type reference layers for annotation and validation workflows.
