// Mission

Why we built
EcoViewer

Ecological characterisation at scale requires consistent, high-quality reference data. EcoViewer was built to bridge the gap between raw satellite imagery and meaningful ecological interpretation — providing practitioners and researchers with a unified, multi-source reference layer accessible anywhere in the world, directly in the browser.

Built on Google Earth Engine's planetary-scale infrastructure, EcoViewer removes the technical barriers that traditionally limited access to high-resolution environmental context data.
