Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

EcoViewer is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

EcoViewer does not collect personal information directly. However, as the Service is built on Google Earth Engine, your use of EcoViewer is subject to Google's data collection practices. This may include:

  • Authentication information when you sign in with your Google account
  • Usage data collected by Google Earth Engine for quota and abuse monitoring
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information collected by Google

3. Website Analytics

This website may use anonymised analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. No personal data is stored on our servers.

4. Cookies

This website uses essential cookies required for basic functionality. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

5. Google Earth Engine & Third Parties

EcoViewer is hosted on Google Earth Engine's infrastructure. When you use the application, you interact directly with Google's systems. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how your data is handled by their platform.

6. Data Retention

EcoViewer does not store personal data on its own servers. Any data retained by Google Earth Engine is subject to Google's data retention policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete data held about you. As EcoViewer does not directly hold personal data, requests should be directed to Google for Earth Engine-related data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, please contact the EcoViewer team via the Team page.
